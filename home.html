    Where Flora Meets Tech



What Botnomata project do ?


The very nature of our project at the Botnomata, connects us to the Humane aspect of our being.Today environment is at the par level of degradation, so there should be efforts in direction of saving the same by whatever way possible.

Our way for doing the same is better exploration of our natural habitat, by finding the suitable Flower type knowing its Sepal length, Sepal width, Petal length and Petal width input by the Botanist, students with flora majors and so as Machine Learning Enthusiast!

We believe that informatics can help discover the environment more immensly. So we have made a Machine learning application which helps the Scientists discover the crucial aspects behind the classification algorithms like K-Nearst Neighbour and Logistic Regression.
